## Getting Started

### Create Your Account
1. Visit [Milin Shop](https://milinshop.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** at the top right
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Complete your profile with:
   - Full name
   - Phone number (for delivery updates)
   - Delivery address
   - Preferred sizes and colors

### Browse & Search
- Use the **Shop** menu to browse by category
- Filter by size, color, price range
- Search by designer brand or occasion
- Check **"Available"** badge for in-stock items

## How Rental Works

### Step 1: Select Your Item
1. Click on any product to view details
2. Choose your size (refer to our size chart)
3. Select your rental dates (must be at least 3 days)
4. Click **"Add to Cart"**

### Step 2: Review & Checkout
1. Go to your **Shopping Bag** (top right)
2. Review all items and rental dates
3. Click **"Proceed to Checkout"**
4. Confirm delivery address
5. Apply any promo codes (if available)

### Step 3: Payment
1. Enter your card details (secured by Stripe)
2. Pay the rental fee + deposit
3. Confirm order
4. You'll receive a confirmation email

### Step 4: Delivery
- We'll arrange shipment to your address
- Tracking number provided via email
- Typically arrives 2-3 business days

### Step 5: Enjoy & Return
- Wear and enjoy your rental!
- Return via prepaid shipping label (included in package)
- Drop off at any shipping location

## Care & Return

### Before Wearing
1. Inspect item upon arrival
2. Check for any defects or damage
3. Report issues within **24 hours** to support@milinshop.com
4. Keep original packaging

### During Rental
- **Wear normally** - items are meant to be enjoyed!
- Dry clean only (if needed) - use our recommended cleaner
- Avoid extreme activities or contact with colored items
- Remove jewelry when dressing/undressing to prevent snagging

### When Returning
1. **Inspect** the item for any damage you caused
2. **Clean** if visibly soiled (or dry clean)
3. **Pack** in original packaging if possible
4. **Attach** prepaid shipping label
5. **Drop off** at courier location (proof of delivery important!)

### Acceptable Wear
Items will show normal signs of use, which is expected:
- Light creasing
- Minimal fading
- General wear from proper use

### What Happens
1. Upon return, our team inspects each item
2. Any damage is documented with photos
3. You'll receive a **Damage Report** within 48 hours
4. Deposit deduction applied if necessary
5. Refund processed within 7 days

### Dispute Process
1. Contact support@milinshop.com within 48 hours
2. Provide photos of item condition
3. Our team will review and respond within 24 hours
4. Escalation available if needed

### Full Replacement Charge
Items are considered a total loss if:
- Permanent stains (wine, blood, etc.)
- Large tears (>5cm) in visible areas
- Multiple damage points
- Color fading/alteration

In these cases, you'll be charged the full retail value (typically 3-5x daily rental).

## FAQ

### How far in advance can I book?
You can book up to **60 days in advance**. This helps us manage inventory and ensures availability.

### Can I extend my rental?
Yes! If the item is available for the following days, you can request an extension via your account. Extensions are subject to availability.

### What if my item arrives damaged?
Contact us immediately (within 24 hours). Send photos and we'll provide a replacement or refund at no cost. This is on us, not your deposit.

### Can I cancel my booking?
- **Free cancellation**: Up to 7 days before rental start date
- **50% refund**: 3-7 days before start date  
- **No refund**: Within 3 days of rental start

### Do you clean the items?
Yes. Every item is professionally cleaned between rentals. Items arrive fresh and ready to wear.

### What if I need a size exchange?
Contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange return and send the correct size (free shipping both ways).

### Is there a limit to how many items I can rent?
No fixed limit, but extremely large orders may require special processing. Contact us for orders of 10+ items.
